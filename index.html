A long time ago...
June 2019 - September 2020

In my role as Full Stack Developer, I contributed to a platform enabling digital contracts, focusing on features for users to create and sign documents. Utilizing Vue.js and Vuex for the front end and Express with knex and MySQL for the backend, I also gained insights into payment gateway operations. Transitioning to a new project, I became part of a team developing a Jira-integrated application for running retro ceremonies. Responsibilities included understanding add-on development documentation, establishing the initial architecture, and implementing real-time functionality using server-sent events (SSE) and Redis store. We were following the practices of Scrum methodology on a daily basis. This experience enhanced collaborative skills within a dynamic team, and reinforced the importance of a proactive approach in the workplace.

La Solidarité
February 2023 - August 2023

I wanted to try something completely different in my life, unrelated to my profession, aiming to discover an alternative way of life. This led me to volunteer in France, in a charming village called Vaunières, where I worked for the association of Villages des Jeunes. Nestled in the mountains, surrounded by nature, the village serves as a hosting centre for various groups. My responsibilities covered a wide range of tasks, from facilitating group activities, to everyday chores, all the way to preserving the historical heritage of the village. During the almost seven months I spent as a volunteer, I gained invaluable insights into the importance of a collective life.
